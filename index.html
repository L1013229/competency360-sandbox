Why does my organisation need Competency360?

Your organisation should consider using Parallaxx's Competency 360 service in response to the discontinuation of many CoPTTM warrants by NZTA because:

Shift in Responsibility

With the NZTA moving away from centralised competency tracking, the burden of monitoring staff qualifications, competency, and compliance now falls heavily on employers. Competency 360 provides an easy and effective solution for tracking these competencies across your workforce, ensuring you remain compliant without the need for complex internal systems.

What is Competency360 by Parallaxx?

Competency360 is a comprehensive competency management system tailored for Temporary Traffic Management (TTM) roles. It provides a customised competency matrix that aligns with your business needs, allowing you to assess and report on staff competencies against specific role expectations.

Competency Matrix

Develop a TTM competency matrix tailored to your business, roles, and tasks, producing specific reports to evidence competency against set expectations.

Competency Libraries

Access a range of competencies that match roles and functions. Develop them internally with naturally occurring evidence from on-job performance.

Competency Reports

Get individual and team competency reports, tailored by role, function, or environment. These report snapshots show strengths and identify areas needing improvement.

Online Micro-Learning

Self-paced, 10-minute learning modules covering key TTM knowledge areas. Mobile-friendly, engaging, and aligned with competency tracking.

Competency Checks

External validation of competency, ensuring an independent check of skills. This includes external credentials like unit standards.

Competency360 Process

You can use as many or as few of the steps and services as you like. Just want the competency snapshots? Go for it. Just want access to the knowledge hub? Sure. Want to start with Independent Competency Assessments across the business to see where you're at? No problem.

1. Consultation and Needs Assessment

We begin by understanding your unique requirements, roles, functions, tasks, and people.

2. Customised Competency Matrix

We collaborate with you to develop a customised competency matrix for your roles, functions, and people that maps what competencies are needed, and where they can come from (such as external training, on-job learning, internal courses, mentoring and organic practice, self-assessment, etc.).

3. Reporting

We develop the reporting, giving you access to the competency database that matches your roles, functions, tasks, and people so you can see where they are at any time - and so can they.

4. Generate the Competency

Deploying the competency tracking and reporting allows for the competency development process to accelerate, collecting evidence and undertaking on-job training, assessment, and evidence collection so the competency snapshots grow and improve over time.

5. Engage with the Knowledge Hub

Supercharge your people's knowledge and keep it up to date by leveraging our short (<10 minute) micro-courses online that cover key knowledge areas that contribute to the competency snapshots.

6. Independent Assessment

Call us in to help with external validation, auditing, assessment, training, and more.

7. Ongoing Review and Alignment

We conduct annual or bi-annual reviews of your competency matrix and assessments to ensure they stay aligned with your company's evolving needs.
