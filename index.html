Our Competency Services

Competency Analysis

Develop a TTM competency matrix tailored to your business, roles, and tasks, producing specific reports to evidence competency against set expectations.

Competency Libraries

Access a range of competencies that match roles and functions. Develop them internally with naturally occurring evidence from on-job performance.

Competency Snapshots

Get individual and team competency reports, tailored by role, function, or environment. Snapshots show strengths and identify areas needing improvement.

Competency Checks

External validation of competency, ensuring an independent check of skills. This includes external credentials like unit standards.

Online Micro-Learning Modules

Self-paced, 10-minute learning modules covering key TTM knowledge areas. Mobile-friendly, engaging, and aligned with competency tracking.
