With the end of the TTM Warrant system by NZTA, managing TTM competency will need a fresh approach that is easy to deploy and use by companies who employ staff that work on New Zealand's roads.

Competency 360 by Parallaxx provides tailored competency services and reports for individuals and organisations in TTM. Our solution leverages on-job evidence, simple competency assessments, and customised learning to help make competency tracking and assurance easy and accurate.

Why choose Competency 360 by Parallaxx?

Track and Prove Competencies

Collect and use naturally occurring evidence from your workplace to track and prove TTM competencies.

Powerful Competency Reports

Access a database of competencies that builds simple but powerful 2-page reports on your people's competency status, so you can see where they are at and what they are missing.

Enhance Skills with External Resources

Access external micro-learning knowledge topics, as well as external assessments and unit standard audits to gather additional evidence and ensure your people are the best they can be.

Our Process

1. Consultation and Needs Assessment

We begin by understanding your unique requirements, roles, functions, tasks, and people.

2. Competency Analysis and Mapping

We collaborate with you to develop a customised competency map for your roles, functions, and people that maps what competencies are needed, and where they can come from (such as external training, on job learning, internal courses, mentoring and organic practice, self-assessment, etc.).

3. Reporting

We develop the reporting, giving you access to the competency database that matches your roles, functions, tasks, and people so you can see where they are at any time - and so can they.

4. Generate the Competency

Deploying the competency tracking and reporting allows for the competency development process to accelerate, collecting evidence and undertaking on-job training, assessment, and evidence collection so the competency snapshots grow and improve over time.

5. Engage with the Knowledge Hub

Supercharge your people's knowledge and keep it up to date by leveraging our short (<10 minute) micro-courses online that cover key knowledge areas that contribute to the competency snapshots.

6. Independent Assessment

Call us in to help with external validation, auditing, assessment, training, and more.

You can use as many or as few of the steps and services as you like. Just want the competency snapshots? Go for it. Just want access to the knowledge hub? Sure. Want to start with Independent Competency Assessments across the business to see where you're at? No problem.
